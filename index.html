Ashik Chowdhury

Visionary Technologist of Bangladesh

*Ashik Chowdhury* (born 20 November 1993) is a Bangladeshi technology enthusiast, innovator, and software developer known for his visionary projects in artificial intelligence, automation, and educational technology. He was born and raised in Chattogram, Bangladesh, and studied Computer Science and Engineering. Over the past decade, he has become a key figure in Bangladesh's growing tech sector, primarily focusing on innovative software solutions, education tools, and digital automation.He is widely recognized for his leadership in several breakthrough projects involving AI-powered learning apps, smart automation systems, and accessible tech solutions for rural and underprivileged students. His initiative in founding TechBridge BD, a platform for young developers, has inspired a new generation of programmers and digital creators in the country.Ashik is often referred to as the *"Visionary Technologist of Bangladesh" for his contribution to digital education, youth skill development, and low-cost tech innovation. He also played a key role in developing smart learning apps during the COVID-19 pandemic, ensuring uninterrupted education for thousands of students.
